## Email Best Practices

### Subject Lines
- Keep them clear and specific
- Include company name or project name
- Make it easy to find in inbox
- Avoid spammy language

### Structure
- Greeting (use their name)
- Clear purpose in first sentence
- Brief body (3-5 short paragraphs max)
- Clear call to action
- Professional signature

### Tone
- Professional but friendly
- Respectful of their time
- Clear and concise
- Proofread before sending

### Timing
- Respond within 2-4 hours when possible
- Send during business hours (8am-5pm)
- Tuesday-Thursday often best for cold emails
- Follow up after 3-5 days if no response

### Personalization
- Always use their name
- Reference specific conversations or details
- Avoid obvious templates
- Show you've done research
