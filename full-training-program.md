# Shaffer Construction B2B Sales Training Program
## Facilities Maintenance & Commercial Electrical Subcontractor Sales

**Program Duration:** 5 Weeks
**Target Audience:** Electricians transitioning to B2B sales roles
**Focus:** Selling electrical subcontractor services to general contractors and facilities maintenance companies
**Last Updated:** November 5, 2025

---

## Program Overview

This comprehensive training program equips electricians with the skills needed to sell Shaffer Construction's electrical services to general contractors, facilities maintenance companies, and commercial property managers in the Los Angeles area.

### Program Goals
1. Understand the B2B facilities maintenance market
2. Master consultative selling for electrical subcontractor services
3. Build relationships with general contractors and facilities managers
4. Navigate RFPs, bids, and prequalification processes
5. Achieve sales certification within 5 weeks
