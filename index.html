Our Competitive Advantages

1. Triple Licensed (C-10, B, A)

Broader capabilities than C-10-only competitors. Can act as general contractor for electrical-focused projects and access infrastructure work.

2. Small and Nimble

Fast decision-making without bureaucracy, direct access to owners, personalized service, and maximum flexibility.

3. Los Angeles Expertise

Deep knowledge of local permitting, relationships with inspectors, understanding of LA building codes, and no travel fees for local work.

4. Proven Relationships

Current clients (Stratus, PVC, Brite Construction) as references with track record of reliable service.

5. Owner Accessibility

Direct line to decision makers, personal attention to projects, and accountability at the highest level.
